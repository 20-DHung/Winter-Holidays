TOURS IN KYRGYZSTAN

Climbing to the holy and picturesque places in the Karagoy National Park

  • Season : July-September
  • Altitude : 2300 m-3400 m above sea level
  • Track mileage : 25 km
  • Location coordinates : Latitude 40.050958 Longitude 72.6529574

    • TOUR PROGRAM:

    Duration: 5 days

    Day 0

    Meeting at the airport. Transfer to the hotel. Rest until 10.00 .

    Day 1 : Departure towards the Kalday village to the Karagoy

    with a visit to :
    - UNESCO monument, sacred mountain Sulaiman-Too, where we will visit 3 caves: Rushan, Teshik Tash and Kyzyl Unkur .
    Dinner
    After lunch we visit the old Osh bazaar, do some shopping . Dinner, return to the hotel .

    Day 2 : Departure towards the Kalday village to the Karagoy.

    - Forest reserve (1 hour 20 minutes on the way): Accommodation in a guest yurt Ascent, 5 km one way hiking to the sacred peak Mazar-too 3400m, photo panoramas of the surrounding mountains (lunchboxes)
    Descent to the guest yurt.
    Dinner. Overnight in a guest yurt, or in a tent in a picturesque place of your choice .

    Day 3 : Departure towards Lake Karagoy

    - After breakfast at 9:00, departure towards Lake Karagoy, at the fork in the road on footascent along the river valley to the mountain lake Karagoy 6.5 km one wayphoto of panoramas on the lake (lunchboxes)Descent to the forkDeparture to Osh. Dinner. Overnight at the hotel .

    1 day

    Age 0

    Tour Location

    Category : Trekking

    Duration : day trip

    Best Season : May — September

    Difficulty : Moderate, passes at 3 300 meter

    Requirements : Trekking shoes, waterproof clothes, hat, sunscreen & sunglasses.

    Distance : 15 km walk

    DESCRIPTION : Observe wildlife in a protected natural park. Escape the summer heat of the Ferghana Valley. Relax in solitude surrounded by the cool juniper forests of the Kyrgyz Ata National Reserve. The sound of flowing water and birdsong provides a serene environment for hiking along ancient nomadic trails. Keen eyes may spot furry marmots, soaring eagles and other birdlife. Walk in the cool dawn around the summer camps of local Kyrgyz herdsmen busy with milking cows and mares. Spend one (or more) evenings under the wide starry sky and sleep soundly after hours of fun in the fresh mountain air.

    Drive to Kyrgyzata national reserve by car, 2 hours or 75 km. Walk up to the summit of the mountain (3300 m) and down to the shepherd’s yurts (4 hours).

    ITINERARY : Osh city - Nookat village - Kyrgyzata (national park) — Karagoi summer camp - Nookat village - Osh city.
